About Zenith

What is Zenith?

Are you a coder, technocrat, entrepreneur or a creativity digger looking for a challenge? This is an event for coders who have the hacking spirits and love giving life to their ideas.
The goal? You can hack anything that provides a solution to any impactful problem. Channel your focus, build a mobile application or web application and show us your project. Anything that can limit you is your own creativity. Make your disruptive ideas happen.

Register Your Team
  1. Fill up the registration form.
  2. You need to create a team of maximum 3 people and minimum 1.
  3. Submit a summary of your project idea.
Shortlisting

Our panel will review your ideas, profiles and will notify you whether you are shortlisted or not via email and sms

Project Completion Deadline

After successful shortlisting, you need to develop your idea, and submit screenshots of working project to us.

Pitching

You will be called onsite for pitching your project in front of judges.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

If you dont find your problem listed below, feel free to drop us an email manantechnosurge[at]gmail[dot]com

What should i write in the project summary?

Well you are free to choose any kind of idea, any kind of platform. You should write about

  • Your idea
  • How your idea will solve some sort of problem
  • How your idea is scalable or impactful
  • How feasible is your project
  • You don't need to write about yourself or your teams, we will review your profiles to know more about you.

Is there any theme for the hackathon?

No, Comeon you can hack whatever you want.

How will i know whether i am shortlisted or not?

As soon as you will register yourself, our shortlisting panel will reply you back within 3-4 days maximum via call/sms/email, to instruct you about further procedures and an official invite will be delivered to you for joining us at onsite project pitching cum demonstration round.

What if i am not able to complete the project till the presentation day or what if my team is unable to join inperson at the hackathon site?

Well in this case, we would request you to contact us as soon as possible, so that we can cancel your slot and give your slot to the next deserving team.

Will i be provided some kind of transport allowance to come to the hackathon site?

Sorry, we won't be able to provide you with any transport allowance. You need to arrange your travel on your own. Anyways you can contact us to guide you for the cheapest routes.

What all things do i need to bring to the hackathon site?

You need to bring each and every thing that is required to perform a live demonstration of your project in front of judges.

What do i get on attending Zenith?

Pretty much everything from cash prizes and gift cards to socializing with fellow hacker community. And yeah prized worth rupees 1,00,000/-
