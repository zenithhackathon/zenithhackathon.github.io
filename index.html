FAQs

What will be the team size?
This hackathon is strictly a team competition. The team size maximum of five and minimum two is allowed.
What if I don't want to present my hack?
It's necessary to present your hack(even unfinished). Presenting your hack gives you a chance to showcase your idea too.
What are the themes for the hackathon?
The themes for the hackathon will be given on the spot. However, you can expect a wide domain to work upon.
What if I've never been to a hackathon before?
Then we're so glad Zenith will be your first ever! You'll get a great exposure, networking opportunities and last of all - good food. ;-)
Can I start working on my hack before the event?
No, but you can familiarize yourself with all the tools and technologies you intend to use beforehand!
Will my travelling expenses be reimbursed?
Sorry, but we will NOT be able to provide it.
Who will own the IP(Intellectual Property) Rights to the product that I have built?
The developer/developers of the web/mobile application will have all rights and own the IP of the product. However, we promote open source. :D
